Journey of the Hitman

  • March 2005Made his List A debut for West Zone against Central Zone in the Deodhar Trophy at Gwalior, unbeaten innings of 142 in 123 balls against North Zone
  • July 2006made his first-class debut for India A against New Zealand A at Darwin and scored 57 and 22 as India won by 3 wickets.
  • 2006-07Made his Ranji Trophy debut for Mumbai and scored 205 off 267 balls against Gujarat.
  • December 2009 Made his highest career score of 309 not out in the Ranji Trophy against Gujarat.
  • November 2013Sharma made his Test debut at Eden Gardens in Kolkata against the West Indies and scored 177, the second-highest score on debut by an Indian
  • March 2015Sharma made his first appearance in the Cricket World Cup and played in eight matches for India in the 2015 tournament in Australia and scored 330 runs in the tournament with one century, a score of 137 in the quarter-final against Bangladesh.
  • April 2019Sharma was appointed vice-captain of India's squad for the 2019 tournament in England.
  • November 2019He became the first male cricketer for India to play in 100 T20Is
  • November 2020 Sharma was nominated for the ICC Men's ODI and T20I Cricketer of the Decade awards
  • September 2021Sharma had played in 381 international matches (43 Test, 227 ODI, 111 T20I) and scored 41 centuries (8 Test, 29 ODI, 4 T20I).
